Quick troubleshooting

My companion isn't growing.
Open the iPhone app once a day so Anka can read your latest Health data and record a snapshot. If the iPhone app hasn't run in over 24 hours, Anka skips that day.

The Watch complication shows "Open Anka".
Open the iPhone app at least once. The Apple Watch app will then sync your companion within a few seconds.

I bought Anka Premium but it shows as locked on another device.
Tap Settings → Restore Purchase. Apple will verify your purchase via your Apple ID and unlock everything.

I'm not getting notifications.
Make sure notifications are enabled in Settings → Notifications → Anka. Anka never sends notifications between 22:00 and 08:00 by design.

I want to start over.
Tap Settings → Reset Anka in the iPhone app. This deletes your current companion and snapshots. Your Premium unlock and album history remain.

Privacy

Anka reads your Health data on-device only and never transmits it to any server. See the Privacy Policy for details.
