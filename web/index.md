# Anka

**A wrist companion born of myth.**

Anka is an iPhone and Apple Watch game that turns your daily steps,
heartbeats, sleep, and workouts into nourishment for a mythological creature
on your wrist. Five creatures. Five evolution paths. One pure premium
experience — no ads, no subscriptions, no in-app purchases beyond a single
one-time unlock.
