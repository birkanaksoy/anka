Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-25

Anka ("the app", "we", "us") is built and operated by Birkan Aksoy as an independent developer. We take your privacy seriously and have designed Anka to be a privacy-first experience.

What we collect

Nothing leaves your device.

Anka reads the following health data from Apple Health on your iPhone and Apple Watch:

  • Step count
  • Heart rate
  • Stand hours
  • Apple Exercise Time
  • Sleep analysis

This data is read on-device only, used to compute your companion's daily growth, and never transmitted to any server, third party, advertiser, or analytics provider.

We do not collect:

  • Your name, email, phone number, address, or any personal identifier
  • Your precise location, IP address, or device identifiers
  • Any usage analytics that could identify you

App Tracking

Anka does not track you across other companies' apps or websites and does not request App Tracking Transparency permissions.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, Anka may schedule a daily reminder and an evolution celebration. These are generated and delivered locally by your device — no notification content is sent through our servers because we operate no servers.

In-App Purchases

A single one-time purchase, Anka Premium, unlocks all creatures and evolution paths. Purchase processing is handled entirely by Apple via the App Store and StoreKit. Apple's privacy policy governs that transaction. We do not receive your payment information.

Apple Watch and iPhone sync

If both devices are paired, your companion's state is shared between them using Apple's WatchConnectivity framework. This data travels directly between your iPhone and Watch over the encrypted local link. We never see it.

Children

Anka is suitable for ages 4+. We do not knowingly collect any data from anyone of any age, so children are inherently protected by the design of the app.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy we will update the "Last updated" date at the top and, if material, present a notice in the app.
