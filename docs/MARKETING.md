# My Anka — Marketing Strategy

**Positioning:** *Apple Watch native game with iPhone companion* — not the
other way around.

---

## Tagline Hierarchy

- **One-liner:** "A mythic creature lives on your Apple Watch."
- **Subtitle (App Store, 30 char):** "Apple Watch Mythic Companion"
- **Long pitch:** "Steps, heartbeats and sleep evolve an Anatolian myth creature on your wrist. Five paths, one purchase, no subscriptions."

---

## Where to Launch

Tier 1 — Watch-native channels:

- **r/AppleWatch** (1.5M members)
- **r/AppleWatchFans**
- **r/IndieDev** + r/iosgaming with "Apple Watch game" framing
- **Watch-focused YouTubers:** iJustine, MaxTech, Quinn Nelson (Snazzy Labs)
- **Watch blogs:** AppleWatchGames.com, Apple Watch Today, Wareable
- **MacRumors Watch forum**
- **iMore Apple Watch section** (often features indie Watch apps)

Tier 2 — Mythology + niche:

- **r/mythology**
- **r/turkish** (Anatolian creatures angle)
- Premium app aggregators: AppShopper, Class Apps, Federico Viticci's MacStories

Tier 3 — Broad:

- Product Hunt (Apple Watch tag)
- Twitter/X with #AppleWatch hashtag
- TikTok with Watch face screen-recording showing complication

---

## Visual Strategy

**Watch first** in every piece of marketing:

1. Hero shot: Apple Watch on a wrist, Anka complication visible
2. Second shot: Watch app fullscreen with creature breathing
3. iPhone shots come AFTER Watch shots
4. App Store preview video: opens with Watch, transitions to iPhone

Apple Watch sahibi olan kullanıcılara Watch screenshot'ları öncelikli gösterilir (App Store algorithm prefers Watch screenshots when available for Watch-supported apps).

## Conversion Funnel Notes

**Free download apps** rely on retention + IAP conversion.
**Paid apps** ($4.99) rely on:

1. **Search rank** for "apple watch game" / "watch companion" / "tamagotchi watch"
2. **Editor's choice** opportunity (Watch indie premium apps get features)
3. **Word of mouth** — paid apps live or die on this; review screenshots
4. **Press features** — premium $4.99 apps get more press than F2P (cleaner story)

Goal: 1,000 sales in first month = $5K gross = $3.5K net. Stretch: 10K = $35K net.
