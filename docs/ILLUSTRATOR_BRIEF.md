## Project: Anka — Mythological Wrist Companion (iOS / Apple Watch game)

I'm an independent iOS developer looking for an illustrator for my Apple Watch + iPhone game called **Anka**. The game features five mythological creatures from Anatolian / Turkic folklore that evolve based on the player's daily health activity (steps, heart rate, sleep, etc.).

I need a cohesive set of premium creature illustrations that work both on a small Apple Watch screen and on a larger iPhone canvas.

## Style direction

- **Aesthetic:** Premium, hand-drawn, slightly stylized. Think *Studio Ghibli meets Anatolian miniature art*. Not 8-bit, not kawaii, not cartoonish.
- **Mood:** Warm, mythic, slightly mysterious. Not scary.
- **Palette:** Warm metallics (brass, copper, antique gold), deep night blues, dark wood browns, accent colors per creature. Background gradient on the wrist (the app uses a dark workshop palette).
- **Lineart:** Visible but soft. Not heavy black outlines. Slight imperfection / hand quality preferred.
- **Composition:** Each creature centered, front-facing or 3/4 view, occupies ~70% of canvas. Visible silhouette at 40×40 px (Apple Watch complication).

### Creature notes

**Anka (Firebird / Phoenix)** — Turkish/Persian phoenix that burns to ash and rises. Red-gold-orange palette. Wings, long neck. Evolved stage should feel majestic and slightly aflame.

**Şahmaran (Serpent-Queen)** — Anatolian legend: snake body, human female head with crown. Emerald greens, jeweled accents. Wise expression. Symbolic of underground wisdom.

**Hodağ (Dark Forest Guardian)** — Anatolian forest creature, antlered, four-legged. Deep purples, midnight blues. Visible only at night — bioluminescent eyes/markings on evolved form.

**Karakoncolos (Winter Spirit)** — Folk creature of long winter nights. Furry, grey-white-ice-blue. Slightly trickster-ish. Frost details. Evolved form: tall, almost regal.

**Pirebatak (Swift Helper Spirit)** — From Turkish folk tales; small helpful spirit. Earth tones (terracotta, ochre). Fox/rabbit-like. Quick, friendly, agile. Evolved form: graceful, almost dancer-like.

### Egg
A single shared egg design — textured, warm-lit, slightly worn. Hint of brass / golden detail. Used for all five creatures' starting stage.

### Stage progression
- **Egg:** the shared egg
- **Hatchling:** baby version, large eyes, soft proportions
- **Young:** mid-growth, recognizable but not final
- **Adult:** full stature, before specialization
- **Evolved:** the creature's "true form" — most dramatic / mythic

## App icon

a) Feature one creature (preferably **Anka the firebird**) as a silhouette / silhouette-and-flame, OR
b) An abstract mythic mark — moon + flame, eye + crown, etc.

I lean toward **(a) — a stylized Anka silhouette with flame**, framed by the gold and dark palette.
